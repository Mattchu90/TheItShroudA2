Jackson Shepherd - Director of IT Operations

My ideal job - Director of IT Operations - is defined as someone who looks after and maintains the IT environment in a workplace. The employee who holds this job should be able to not only maintain but introduce new technologies to further improve and develop the business to make operations more efficient. This type of job seems to be quite popular when referencing to the Burning glass data. A system administrator, which is a key IT skill that is requried by an IT operations manager, seems to scale quite high in employer searches with 681 postings in March of 2017 to March of 2018.

Keeping the Burning Glass data in mind, my idea job has not changed. I believe that with the popularity that the systems administrator comes with, it will be easier to find a job with the title similar to the IT Operations manager as well as having not only the skills (IT or non-IT) but also having the experience that I am gaining through my current job.
